January Jaunt

Navigational Car Rally


 

 

2013 January Jaunt Results

The 2013 January Jaunt was the first spring rally of the year. With temperatures over 14°C, competitors battled with mud instead of snow and ice: no one went home without the need for a long car wash!

With a field of 18 competitors, the 15 Novices had a real competition on their hands, competing not only with each other, but with challenging instructions as well. There was a large field of first- and second-time rallyists, competing alongside some more experienced novices, and at the same time trying to understand instruction types like clock faces, tulips constructed from map segments, and the ever-confusing straight-line segments. The Jaunt is a long navigational rally, and it did take its toll.

Awards in Novice went to the teams of Boudreau and Boudreau in first place, Olmstead and Hooper in second place and Armstrong and Deathe in third place.

First Novices Boudreau and Boudreau

 

Second Novices Olmstead and Hooper

Third Novices Armstrong and Deathe

 

Intermediates and Experts had a more challenging set of instructions (the same for both classes) including 4 mapping sections. Intermediates Roger Sanderson and Linda Connelly topped the Intermediate class, while the team of Louis Cabardos and Brian Maxwell scored an impressive 0.9 points over 3 legs of tough competition!

 

First Intermediates Sanderson and Connelly

First Experts and Overall Winners Cabardos and Maxwell

 

As organizer, I’d like to thank everyone who helped make event day a success. This included all checkpoint workers: Fred Walker and Jane Worobess (also registration), Rob Jones and Janet Tse, Christina Chinn and Gary Tomlinson, Paul Henshall, Trevor Hancher, Polina Luchun and scorers Martin Loveridge, Dennis Wharton and Steve Van Rees. Also thanks to greencrew Bruce Leonard and Trevor Hancher, Evelyn Seelenmayer for assistance with instructions, and co-organizer Dietmar Seelenmayer. And, of course, everyone who decided to compete in the event – hopefully everyone enjoyed the event, and is willing to give it a try again in the future!

Kurt Seelenmayer

Organizer, 2013 January Jaunt
